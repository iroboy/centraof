About the company

Yacht Popilov Shipbuilding Company

The Russian shipyard Yachts Popilov was founded in 2013. The inspiration and founder of the shipyard is its permanent director, Andrei Nikolaevich Popilov.

Today, the company is a leader in the market for steel trawler-type yachts. We build boats for those who value comfort, reliability and freedom for those who want a real home on the water, ready for life and travel in any season. At sea, as at home this is the philosophy that the entire team at Yachts Popilov strictly adheres to.

The vessels are reliable and easy to operate, equipped with various systems from well-known manufacturers of marine equipment.

Today, the shipyard produces yachts 15, 16, 20 and 36 metres long, as well as catamarans of its own design. All models are expedition class, with enhanced seaworthiness and designed for long-term autonomous sailing. Custom-built yachts and catamarans are available upon request.

The shipyard carries out a full cycle of work, from concept and design documentation to implementation and launch. The hulls of the yachts are made of reliable and durable marine steel, a material that can withstand the harsh conditions of the northern seas and the equatorial climate.
The company's own furniture production allows it to offer customers interior personalisation, taking into account the style and wishes of the owner.

The shipyard strictly controls the production deadlines for yachts. At each stage, from welding during hull construction, painting, installation of ship systems and equipment, to furniture assembly, we ensure quality control of the work performed.

Marina development solutions from Yachts Popilov

A reliable marina is not just infrastructure, it is an extension of our quality guarantee. We offer comprehensive engineering solutions for the construction and modernisation of yacht marinas, created with attention to detail and high quality standards. We manufacture key elements for marina development.

Pontoons

The basis for safe and comfortable mooring. Due to their weight, concrete pontoons are stable in all weather conditions, minimising the risk of damage to hulls during mooring.

Special concrete grades and manufacturing technologies ensure exceptional resistance to salt water, ice and abrasion.

Pontoons are easily configured to any marina size and layout, ensuring maximum efficiency of water area use.

Dead anchors

Designed and manufactured to ensure maximum reliability of pontoon systems even under extreme loads (storms, ice).

They eliminate the risk of pontoon drift, ensuring the stability of mooring places and the safety of neighbouring yachts.

Special concrete guarantees the safety and functionality of anchors throughout the entire service life of the marina.
